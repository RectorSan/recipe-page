Spaghetti Bolognese

A classic Italian pasta dish made with a rich, hearty meat sauce. Perfect for a comforting meal!

Recipe Details

Prep Time: 10 minutes

Cook Time: 20 minutes

Total Time: 30 minutes

Servings: 6

Difficulty: Easy

Dish Image

Ingredients

  • 8 ounces spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Add ground beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart as it cooks.
  4. Stir in chopped onions and garlic; cook until softened.
  5. Add marinara sauce and Italian seasoning, then mix well.
  6. Season with salt and pepper, reduce heat, and simmer for 10 minutes.
  7. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti and enjoy.

Tips

You can add grated parmesan, fresh basil, or red pepper flakes for extra flavor.
